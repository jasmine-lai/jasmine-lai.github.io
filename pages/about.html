about me

a short introduction

hi! i'm jasmine. i'm from acton, ma, but currently living in boston. i'm a second-year student at northeastern university studying computer science. i've always been excited about working in a development field and pushing the boundaries of society, especially technology.

here are some of my interests outside of academics:
i've been figure skating since i was 6 years old, and i competed recreationally in high school and competed at the collegiate level during my first year at northeastern. these days, skating is more of a fun and nostalgic personal hobby rather than a competitive goal, but i still return the rink every so often to practice.
i enjoy going hiking all-year round (check out my hiking photos on the misc page!). i mostly hike in the white mountains in new hampshire, but i'd hike all around the world given the opportunity. my dream is to hike in patagonia. to date, i've completed the AMC list of 48 4000-footer mountains in the whites, as well as the presidential traverse. i also completed the tour du mont blanc (TMB) in july 2022.
i love to travel. i think that learning about other countries' history and culture is an important aspect of life. i've been to 26 countries and 26 states in the US. my top three favorite countries are norway, iceland, and switzerland.
in the winter, i am an avid snowboarder. my favorite mountains to board at would have to be vail, colorado and park city, utah. i also recently got into bouldering at local gyms with friends.
i'm an avid genshin impact fan! i'm really curious about the development of the game, from the roadmap for the entire game to the process for implementing new features for each patch. genshin has also inspired me to start creating music again (i used to play piano in high school).
